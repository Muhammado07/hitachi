Al Salem Johnson Controls

Al Salem Johnson Controls (ASJC) is a leading “one-stop-destination” for HVAC&R Systems, Controls Systems, Building Management Systems, Security, Safety & Fire Systems, and Energy Services, optimum for the Residential, Commercial & Industrial Sectors, in Saudi Arabia, Lebanon, Egypt & Yemen. In 2019, ASJC entered into a distribution agreement that led to adding some of HITACHI’s cooling solutions to our World’s leading trusted brands in the industry, helping ASJC to be a key player in making the future more productive, secure and more sustainable, through added value Integrated Customized Solutions.

Living Harmoniously

People bring purpose to spaces, and we bring comfort to those spaces, allowing people to work live and play to their fullest. Our cooling solutions and services are designed and engineered to the most stringent of requirements, ensuring they remain in efficient and perfect harmony with the demand of today's customers and urban spaces.

Our purpose

Dependable cooling, at the press of the button, is part of what makes life possible and pleasant in Saudi Arabia. We seek to establish 'Living Harmony' between our customers and their environment, creating a balance between work and play, comfort and freedom, through a range of well-designed technologies, stringently engineered to Hitachi's highest standards.
